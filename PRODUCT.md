# Product

## Register

product

## Users

Users are people configuring or using a local AI Companion experience across home, chat, moments, memory, and profile surfaces. They are reading, chatting, reviewing memories, and adjusting persona/worldview content in a quiet companion app rather than browsing a marketing site.

## Product Purpose

This product provides a configurable AI Companion template with avatar, theme, chat, moments, memory, and profile pages. Success means the existing workflows remain clear and usable while the Jiang Yan / Yanyun theme gives the app a mature, atmospheric identity.

## Brand Personality

Restrained, cold-edged, and elegant. The companion should feel like a composed Jianghu martial expert in a moonlit Yanyun world: calm at first glance, sharp on closer inspection, never cute or flashy.

## Anti-references

Do not make this look like a landing page, cyberpunk dashboard, neon game launcher, glassmorphism tech demo, cute companion app, gold-luxury period drama, or over-decorated ancient-style skin. Avoid large new modules, icon reinterpretation, loud animation, bright gradients, white icon backplates, and modern oversized rounded cards.

## Design Principles

- Preserve the real app structure; visual upgrades must support the five existing pages and their current information hierarchy.
- Content reads first; atmosphere is discovered second.
- Use a unified product component vocabulary for cards, buttons, fields, tabs, and selected states.
- Let the Yanyun game-world feeling come from palette, paper surfaces, restrained ink details, and character art rather than decorative clutter.
- Keep all major surfaces calm, readable, and mobile-safe.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

Target readable contrast for body text and controls, visible focus states, restrained motion, and clear selected/active states. Background art and corner decoration must stay low-contrast enough not to interfere with text.
